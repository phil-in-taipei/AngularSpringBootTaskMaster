Website Information

Task Master was conceived as a platform to enable freelance workers to better manage their daily lives.


This platform enables users to schedule one-off tasks as well as to create "task schedulers" and "task groups" that automate the scheduling of regularly-occuring tasks.


Users can apply the schedulers to specific quarters/years to implement bulk scheduling of tasks in a time-saving and efficient manner.


Choose between daily or monthly displays of tasks and easily confirm task completion or reschedule tasks.
